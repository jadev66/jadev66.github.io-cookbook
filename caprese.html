Caprese Salad

Caprese salad is a classic Italian dish known for its simplicity and fresh flavors. It is best enjoyed fresh, allowing the flavors of the tomatoes, mozzarella, basil, and olive oil to shine through. It's a perfect dish for summer or any time you want a light and refreshing salad! Here's a traditional Caprese salad recipe.

Ingredients

  • 2-3 large ripe tomatoes, sliced into 1/4-inch thick rounds
  • 1-2 balls of fresh mozzarella cheese, sliced into 1/4-inch thick rounds
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • Extra virgin olive oil
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper, to taste
  • Balsamic vinegar (optional)

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Ingredients
    • Slice the tomatoes and mozzarella into rounds of equal thickness, about 1/4 inch thick.
    • Arrange the tomato slices on a serving platter, alternating with slices of fresh mozzarella cheese.
  2. Assemble the Salad
    • Tuck fresh basil leaves between the tomato and mozzarella slices. You can tear the basil leaves or leave them whole, depending on your preference.
  3. Seasoning
    • Drizzle extra virgin olive oil over the tomatoes and mozzarella slices. You can also drizzle a little balsamic vinegar for added flavor, if desired.
    • Season the salad with salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste.
  4. Serve
    • Serve the Caprese salad immediately as a light appetizer or side dish.

Variations:

  • Caprese Skewers: Thread cherry tomatoes, mini mozzarella balls, and small basil leaves onto skewers. Drizzle with olive oil and balsamic glaze for a fun and portable version.
  • Caprese Salad with Pesto: Layer sliced tomatoes and mozzarella on a plate, then drizzle with homemade or store-bought pesto sauce. Garnish with fresh basil leaves.
  • Caprese Pasta Salad: Toss cooked pasta (like fusilli or penne) with diced tomatoes, mozzarella cubes, fresh basil, olive oil, balsamic vinegar, salt, and pepper for a satisfying pasta salad.
