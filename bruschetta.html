Classic Tomato Bruschetta

This recipe makes a wonderful appetizer or snack. Enjoy!

Ingredients

  • 4-5 ripe tomatoes, diced
  • 1-2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup fresh basil leaves, chopped
  • 2 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper, to taste
  • 1 baguette or Italian bread, sliced
  • Olive oil, for brushing
  • 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar (optional)

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. In a bowl, combine the diced tomatoes, minced garlic, chopped basil, olive oil, balsamic vinegar (if using), salt, and pepper. Mix well and set aside to let the flavors meld.
  3. Slice the baguette or Italian bread into 1/2-inch thick slices. Place the slices on a baking sheet in a single layer.
  4. Brush the bread slices lightly with olive oil on both sides.
  5. Bake in the preheated oven for about 5-7 minutes, or until the bread is golden and crisp.
  6. Remove the toasted bread slices from the oven and let them cool slightly.
  7. Spoon the tomato mixture generously onto each slice of toasted bread.
  8. Serve immediately and enjoy your delicious homemade bruschetta!
