Fruit and Yogurt Parfait

This fruit and yogurt parfait is not only tasty but also packed with nutrients, making it a versatile and satisfying dish for any time of the day! Hereâ€™s a simple recipe to create a delicious fruit and yogurt parfait.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup Greek yogurt (plain or flavored, such as vanilla)
  • 1 cup mixed fresh fruits (such as strawberries, blueberries, raspberries, or mango), chopped if necessary
  • 1/2 cup granola
  • Honey or maple syrup (optional, for added sweetness)
  • Fresh mint leaves, for garnish (optional)

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Yogurt
    • If using plain Greek yogurt, you can sweeten it slightly by stirring in honey or maple syrup to taste. If using flavored yogurt like vanilla, you may not need additional sweetener.
  2. Prepare the Fruits
    • Wash and prepare the fruits as needed.
    • Chop larger fruits like strawberries or mango into bite-sized pieces.
  3. Assemble the Parfait
    • Start with a layer of Greek yogurt at the bottom of a glass or bowl.
    • Add a layer of mixed fruits on top of the yogurt.
    • Sprinkle a layer of granola over the fruits.
  4. Repeat Layers
    • Repeat the layers of yogurt, fruits, and granola until you reach the top of the glass or bowl, finishing with a final layer of yogurt.
  5. Garnish
    • Garnish the top with a few pieces of fresh fruit and mint leaves for a decorative touch.
  6. Serve
    • Serve the fruit and yogurt parfait immediately as a nutritious and delicious breakfast or snack.

Tips:

  • Variations:You can customize your parfait with different types of yogurt (regular or dairy-free), various fruits, nuts, seeds, or even a drizzle of nut butter.
  • Make Ahead: Parfaits can be assembled ahead of time and stored in the refrigerator for a few hours. Add the granola just before serving to maintain its crunchiness.
