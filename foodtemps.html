Food Temperature Chart

The FDA (Food and Drug Administration) provides guidelines on safe food temperatures to prevent foodborne illnesses. Here are some recommended minimum internal temperatures for various types of food:

  1. Beef, Pork, Lamb, and Veal (steaks, chops, roasts): 145°F (63°C) with a 3-minute rest time
  2. Ground Meats (beef, pork, lamb, veal): 160°F (71°C)
  3. Poultry (chicken, turkey, duck): 165°F (74°C)
  4. Fish & Shellfish: 145°F (63°C) or until flesh is opaque and separates easily with a fork
  5. Eggs: Cook until yolks and whites are firm, no recommended temperature, but avoid runny eggs for at-risk populations
  6. Leftovers and Casseroles: 165°F (74°C)
