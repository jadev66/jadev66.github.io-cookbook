Twice Baked Potatoes

Twice baked potatoes are a delicious side dish that combines the creamy texture of mashed potatoes with the satisfying crunch of a baked potato skin. Here's a recipe for twice baked potatoes with shredded cheddar cheese, sour cream, and chives.

Ingredients

  • 4 large russet potatoes
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1/2 cup sour cream
  • 2-3 tablespoons butter
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Chopped fresh chives, for garnish

Instructions

  1. Bake the Potatoes
    • Preheat your oven to 400째F (200째C).
    • Scrub the potatoes clean and pierce them several times with a fork to allow steam to escape during baking.
    • Place the potatoes directly on the oven rack and bake for about 45-60 minutes, or until they are tender when pierced with a fork.
    • The exact baking time will depend on the size of your potatoes.
  2. Prepare the Filling
    • Once the potatoes are baked and cool enough to handle, slice each potato in half lengthwise.
    • Scoop out the insides of the potatoes into a large mixing bowl, leaving a thin shell of potato flesh inside the skins.
    • Mash the potato flesh with a potato masher or fork until smooth.
  3. Mix the Fillings
    • Add the shredded cheddar cheese, sour cream, butter, salt, and pepper to the mashed potatoes.
    • Mix well until everything is evenly combined and creamy.
    • Adjust seasoning to taste.
  4. Refill the Potato Skins
    • Spoon the mashed potato mixture back into the potato skins, dividing it evenly among them.
    • Pile it up slightly on top.
  5. Bake Again
    • Place the filled potato halves on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper or foil for easy cleanup.
    • Bake in the oven at 400째F (200째C) for about 15-20 minutes, or until the tops are lightly golden and the filling is heated through.
  6. Serve
    • Remove the twice baked potatoes from the oven and sprinkle with chopped fresh chives for garnish.
    • Serve hot as a delicious side dish or even as a main course with a side salad.
    • Enjoy your cheesy, creamy twice baked potatoes with the savory flavors of cheddar cheese, sour cream, and chives!
